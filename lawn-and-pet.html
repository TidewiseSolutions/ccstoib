The Science Behind Invisible Craftsmanship

Why a CCST Installation
Behaves Differently

Generic artificial grass sits on top of your problems. We engineer them out from underneath.

01

3-Inch Deep Excavation

We completely strip away 3 full inches of unpredictable, sandy topsoil, surface weeds, and unstable root systems — creating a deeply recessed, precision-leveled blank slate.

02

Heavy Geotextile Fabric

An industrial-strength, porous geotextile barrier locks out subterranean weeds and burrowing pests permanently, while allowing liquid to pass through effortlessly.

Premium crushed granite fines, dynamically compacted into a rock-solid, ultra-porous architectural sub-base that will never sag or shift — even after a direct hurricane hit.
