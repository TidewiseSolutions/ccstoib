Our Foundation

Built From the Bedrock Up

The lifespan and beauty of synthetic turf are decided entirely by the structural integrity beneath the surface. Our signature multi-layer engineering process — removing 3 inches of topsoil, deploying commercial-grade geotextile fabric, and mechanically compacting a solid base of premium granite fines — guarantees a flawless, stable foundation. We build outdoor spaces to withstand coastal storms, scorching summers, and heavy daily traffic without shifting, sagging, or fading.
