Architectural Base Precision

A Flawless Roll Starts
Three Inches Underground

A true-roll surface depends entirely on the invisible engineering beneath the turf matrix.

01

3-Inch Deep Excavation

We clear away 3 inches of native, shifty coastal topsoil and weed systems — leaving a deeply stable, cleared subgrade prepared for custom contouring and break design.

02

Heavy Geotextile Fabric

An industrial structural stabilizing fabric is deployed over the bare grade — an ironclad barrier against invasive root structures while preserving full fluid passage.

03

Compacted Granite Fines

Premium crushed granite fines meticulously sculpted into custom breaks and slope variations, then compacted into a permanent, high-porosity sub-base that holds its shape for decades.

Engineered for True Performance

PGA-Tier Check & Drainage

Because of this rock-solid crushed granite foundation, heavy coastal stormwater drains instantly through the turf matrix. The green stays perfectly firm and ready to grab a checking chip shot from the fringe mere moments after a tropical cloudburst.
